About Me

Hello! My name is Alya Rahmatillah Machmud, and I am passionate about a variety of creative and technical fields. While I am deeply invested in web development, I am also experienced in graphic design, branding, UI/UX, and photography. These diverse skills allow me to craft visually compelling and user-centered digital experiences.

Web Development: My web development expertise enables me to build dynamic, responsive websites. I enjoy working with modern web technologies and aim to create intuitive and engaging user interfaces that blend functionality with aesthetics.

Branding & Graphic Design: As a graphic designer, I specialize in branding and editorial design. I love helping brands create visual identities that communicate their values and resonate with their target audience. From logos to editorial layouts, I ensure every element aligns with the overall brand strategy.

UI/UX Design: With a keen interest in user experience design, I focus on creating interfaces that are not only beautiful but also easy to navigate. My goal is to enhance user satisfaction by improving the usability, accessibility, and enjoyment of interacting with products and services.

Photography: As a photographer, I capture moments that tell a story. I believe that good photography can convey powerful messages and evoke emotions. My portfolio includes street photography, landscapes, and portraits, all showcasing my love for composition and detail.

By combining these skills, I aim to create holistic digital solutions that are visually stunning and highly functional, whether it's for a website, a brand identity, or a photo series.
